Design clean, text-free, full-bleed front-cover artwork for a serious lecture-note volume on classical mechanics from Leonard Susskind's Theoretical Minimum course.

This must be flat cover art only, not a photographed or rendered physical book, not a mockup, not a poster, and not a YouTube thumbnail.

Visual direction:
- Japanese editorial minimalism, calm, exact, and rigorous
- focus on one clear classical-mechanics idea only: action, phase-space flow, central-force orbit, pendulum geometry, or elegant mechanical motion
- crisp and deliberate linework with strong line hierarchy
- simpler is better than busy; fewer curves, fewer overlays, more clarity
- reserve broad quiet negative space in the upper portion for later LaTeX title overlay

Palette:
- warm paper, charcoal, muted deep blue, soft gray, with one restrained gold accent

Composition:
- portrait 5:7 for a full printed page
- edge-to-edge cover art
- coherent central structure, not collage clutter
- clean shapes, clean curves, readable spacing

Absolutely avoid:
- any text, letters, numbers, logos, or pseudo-typography
- fake equations, dense plotting noise, or messy graph tangles
- watermarks, stock-photo clichés, classroom screenshots, or 3D product shadows
